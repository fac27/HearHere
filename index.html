How to play

Welcome to HearHere, the game that lets you travel the world through your ears! Can you guess which country a mysterious sound recording comes from? Let's find out!

  1. You're presented with two flags and a mysterious field recording.
  2. Make a guess right away for a chance to win 5 points. Or sacrifice a point to listen to another recording.
  3. You can listen to up to 5 recordings before making a guess.
